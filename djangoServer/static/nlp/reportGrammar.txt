S -> REPORT | REPORT S
REPORT -> QUESTION
QUESTION -> WHICHQ | HOWQ | WHATQ | QBODY
QBODY -> SUBJECT | SUBJECT MODIFIER
SUMBJECT -> ENTITY
MODIFIER -> REPORTKEY | RELATIONSHIP | ATTRIBUTE
WHICHQ -> WPS IS QBODY
HOWQ -> WRB IS QBODY
WHATQ -> WDT IS QBODY
IS -> BE | BED | BEDZ | BER | BEZ
ENTITY -> COMPANY
REPORTKEY ->  CASHFLOW | BALANCESHEET | RATIO | INCOMESTATEMENT
